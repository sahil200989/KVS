The Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (Central Schools Organisation) is a system of central government schools in India governed by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. As of April 2023, it has a total of 1,253 schools in India, and three abroad, in Kathmandu, Moscow and Tehran. It is one of the world's largest chains of schools and the largest chain of schools in India. It is controlled by 25 Regional Offices and 05 ZIETs (Zonal Institute of Education and Training) under the KVS headquarters. In April 2022, the Centre decided to remove an MP quota for KV students, invalidating parliamentarians' recommendations for admission to the schools
